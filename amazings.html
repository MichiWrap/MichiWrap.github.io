The Amazings

Superhero Runner

2014


The Amazings is a runner where kids can play as young super heroes. These heroes all have specific asthma triggers and as the user progresses through the game, they learn more about the strains of asthma, what can trigger asthma, and how to treat and avoid these triggers.

Roles and Responsibilities

Character Design


The Challenge: To concept four unique characters, but in a way that the 3d production team could efficiently and quickly make them.


The Solution: I concepted these characters in a way where they have the same body structure and very similar outfit structures, but differentiate in minor shapes and mostly in hair and colors. This way, our 3d modelers were able to make each character in a quicker manner by reusing parts sculpted from the previously made character.


I concepted each environment within the game in a modular fashion. These environments also reflect the area in which you may come across the asthma hazards that appear within the game.


Comic Pages


Comic book pages are used to segway the game levels together and to help tell the story of these super heroes. I was in charge of concepting and creating these pages.
