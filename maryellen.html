Maryellen's Nifty '50s Scrapbook

Interactive Scrapbook

2015


Maryellen's Nifty '50s Scrapbook is an interactive book that takes the user through the road trip of American Girl's character Maryellen. The postcards, photos, and notes are meant to give kids insight on how things were in the southeast during the 1950's while still providing a fun sense of interactivity.

Roles and Responsibilities

Environment Design


The Challenge: To create a scrapbook that looked like it was made by a girl in the 1950's and to make the minigame backgrounds feel relatable to the scrapbook.


The Solution: I researched the look and feel of scrapbooks during that time period and used crayon-like drawings and scribbles to add a youthful look. This way, the mini games also look like drawing on the scrapbook that comes to life.


UI Design


The Challenge: To two sets of UI -- one that blends with the concept of the scrapbook to enhance UX, and another that acts as an overall UI.


The Solution: The scrapbook UI is treated as the environment is. The UI is masked as postcards, photos, and drawings. While the second type is minimal, clean buttons to not draw too much attention, but also be understandable in purpose.
