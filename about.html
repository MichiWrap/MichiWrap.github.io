About

Anita Paredes is an exceptional artist with over 4 years experience in making art within the gaming industry.  She specializes in Digital Illustrations, Narratives, and Game-related Concepts and Graphics. With her strong work ethic, Anita has taken leading art roles in projects such as Toy Escape, Rewind, and Alphabet Stories.
