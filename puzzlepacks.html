Disney Puzzle Packs

Digital Activity Book

2013 - 2015


Disney Puzzle Packs is a digital activity book where users can choose themes based off of the various Disney franchises and complete puzzles and activities. These activities include, but are not limited to, jigsaw puzzles, spot the difference, memory, and word searches.

Roles and Responsibilities

Wireframes


In the early production of this app for Disney, I was placed in charge of designing wireframes for the various puzzles.

Art Implementation


As explained, Disney provided official art for this project. However, I was in charge of editing and altering the raw art files so that they may be implemented for the various mini games.

Animations


Each minigame begins with an instructional animation on how to play. I was in charge of compiling art and animating those videos.
