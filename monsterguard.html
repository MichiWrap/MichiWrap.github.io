Monster Guard

Emergency Preparedness App

2014


Monster Guard was an app made for The American Red Cross. It uses fun quirky characters to engage kids in a simulation experience as a means of preparing them for emergencies such as fires, earthquakes, floods, and more.

Roles and Responsibilities

Character Design


I concepted the characters seen within the game and lead the 3d art team on bringing these designs to life.

Environment Design


The Challenge: To create an environment that is relatable to the real world, but distant enough to keep the serious topics still light-hearted and not scary.


The Solution: I concepted a simulation like house to create a relatable but distant environment. All hazards and disasters are designed like cardboard cut-outs to create and understandable but non-threatening obstacles.


Animations


I storyboarded and compiled the intro and outro animations for each episode with the help of our music and 3d team.
