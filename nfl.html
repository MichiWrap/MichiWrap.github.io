NFL PLay 60

Endless Realtime Runner

2013-2016


NLF Play 60 is a collaborative project with the NFL and the American Heart Association. This endless runner is meant to encourage and educate kids about health and fitness. The user must run, jump, and turn in real life to have their player run, jump, and turn respectively.

Roles and Responsibilities

Character Design


In the latest 2016-2017 update of NFL Play 60, I was in charge of concepting new characters and modeling them in 3D. In previous installments, I merely assisted with initial concepts.

Advertisement


During the 2015-2016 update I was placed in charge of creating ad images of various sizes for the endless runner section of the game.
