Alphabet Stories

Interactive Reading Learner

2015


Alphabet Stories is a preschool app that helps kids with reading comprehension. There are interactive stories with mini games that have kids spell, match, rhyme, and categorize. All minigames relate to the stories being told, to help enforce reading comprehension.

Roles and Responsibilities

Character Design


I lead the character design team and oversaw concepts and approved them for production.

Environment Design


The Challenge: To create 2-dimensional environment assets that are story-like and can pan in a continuous motion but contain variety.


The Solution: I designed painterly modular assets with one tileable grass asset. This way, we could set up various background scenes with repeating assets, but it still flowed smoothly and felt varied.


UI Design


The Challenge: To create UI that would be clear and understandable to a young child.


The Solution: I designed the UI in the same style as the background and used very defined visual designs rather than text.


Advertising


The screenshots and promotional images for the itunes app and google play store were designed by me.
