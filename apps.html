Apps and Games

Dreamkind and client projects


  • ToyEscape

    Dreamkind


    Toy Escape is an endless slingshot arcade game. In this project I took another leading art role where I was in charge of all the 2d art, character designs and creation, UI/UX, animation, and many particle effects.

  • Rewind

    Dreamkind


    Rewind is a narrative-driven experience, and it was also the first project where I took a leading art role. I was trusted as the only 2d art on this project, so all of the concept art, storyboarding, UI/UX design, 2d graphics, and even the mini game design were my responsibilities. I worked closely with our 3d artist by reviewing all cut scenes and animations. I also worked closely with our programmers to make sure effects and transitions worked properly.

  • Alphabet Stories

    Dreamkind


    Alphabet Stories is a preschool app that teaches kids reading and writing. Towards the end of Rewind's development, I was given the opportunity to work on Alphabet Stories as a lead role, and with that I completely revamped the look and feel of this app. I was given the same responsibilities here as I was Rewind, but since most of this game includes 2d art, I was able to put my own hand into this project more than I could with any previous ones.

  • Maryellen's Nifty '50s Scrapbook

    American Girl


    Maryellen's Scrapbook is an interactive experience that follows American Girl's latest character, Maryellen Larkin. For this project, I was responsible for UI/UX, game design, and 2d graphics. I was trusted as the only artist on this project.

  • Monster Guard

    American Red Cross


    Monster Guard is an app made for the American Red Cross. For this project I was in charge of concept art for characters, environments, in game items, and storyboards. I was not involved with UI/UX in this app, but I was responsible for all the animations aside from the introductory one.

  • The Amazings

    Carolinas HealthCare Systems


    The Amazings is a level-based runner that teaches kids about asthma. This was the first project in which I was given the opportunity to character design. Aside from the that, I also worked on the concepts for the environments, the concepts for the in-game hazards/obstacles, and the artwork within the unlockable comic pages.

  • Disney Puzzle Packs

    Disney


    Disney Puzzle Packs is a digital puzzle book that features many lovable Disney and Pixar characters. This was the first project I was placed on when I began my work with Dreamkind. Most of the art was delivered to us by the client, but I was in charge of wireframes, placement, adjustments, and implementation. Any UI that wasn't provided were my responsibility to create.

  • NFL Play 60

    American Heart Association


    NFL Play 60 is an innovative endless runner that actually requires the player to run in order to control the character. I was given the opportunity to assist the team by creating 2D UI and in character planning in between my work with Puzzle Packs.
